About Us

ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence.

  • Vision

    To contribute to sustainable development of students of LDCE through achieving excellence at par in Research, Innovation, Startups and Entrepreneurship what we call "RISE".

    We visualize the RISE of LDCE by facilitating transformation of students into competent professionals through the support of community at LDCE (L.D. College of Engineering) and ACM (Association for Computing Machinery).

  • Mission

    To impart quality resources and opportunities in order to meet the needs of students, academia and industries to achieve excellence in RISE.

    To connect and collaborate with other academic & research students, startups, institutes as well as industries in order to strengthen educational opportunities and multidisciplinary research.

    To create a conducive research and entrepreneurial ambience that drives innovation and nurtures research-oriented scholars, startups and outstanding professionals.

    To promote equitable and harmonious growth of members, students, academicians, industries and society through networking among Chapter Members, LDCE Students, and ACM.

    To practice and encourage high standards of professional ethics, transparency and accountability as per LDCE and ACM.
